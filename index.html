A b o u t   H Y S T E R I A



The Home of Alt-Comedy in Dublin


Hysteria comedy club is the best place in Dublin to see Ireland's most hilarious comedians. Whether it's at our headline Hysteria show every Friday, our open mic on Thursday or our theme nights every Saturday you are guaranteed to laugh until little drops of something come out of your eyes.

We've got stand-up, improv, drag and podcasts. The whole comedy universe in a lovely 50 seater room downstairs at Sin É, just across the Liffey from Temple Bar.

At Hysteria we prides ourselves for mixing the biggest name in Irish comedy with some of the stars of tomorrow. Our acts have appeared on:

  • Live at The Apollo
  • Conan
  • 8 Out of 10 Cats
  • The Deirdre O'Kane Show
  • The Late Show
  • Clear History
  • The Republic of Telly

A b o u t   S i n   É


Dublin's Original Live Venue

Having first opened it’s doors back in 2001, Sin É has been through many incarnations over the years, all the while establishing itself as one of Dublin cities most eclectic night spots and alternative live music venues.

The venue offers free admission to live music seven nights a week from local and international touring bands, as well as a huge selection of specialist craft ales, draft beer and top shelf liquor to please all tastes.
